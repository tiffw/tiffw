About

My journey from corporate America to startups!

  • 2012

    Jumped on Startup Bus

    With the idea in mind that I wanted to start a startup, I jumped on Startup Bus to pitch my idea to a team, build a product while on the bus to SXSW and three days later pitch to investors at SXSW.

  • 2013

    Launched Roomhints

    Met my co-founder for Roomhints. We bonded at the obscurity of the idea and felt compelled to do the opposite of all trends that were happening within the space. First version of Roomhints included a computer vision matching algorithm to help you find the right item to purchase for your home. Roomhints within the first week got over 30,000 downloads and was featured by Apple. We were off to a good start.

  • 2015

    Moved from NYC to SF

    To continue with building Roomhints and further development my skillset as a startup enterpreneur, I moved to San Francisco and enrolled in UC Berkeley Full Stack Developer program.

  • July 2017

    AR & VR

    Chosen to speak at the Augmented Reality World Expo on the future of shopping and augmented reality.
